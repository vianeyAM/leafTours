Cork

A beautiful breezy city and laid-back vibe, Cork is located on the south coast and is the second largest county in Ireland with the population of 210,000. This cosy city has monumental architecture and infrastructure. The expansion of this county has been growing and making itself known on the map for it’s housing the biggest players in the market such as Apple Inc, Pepsi and other IT giants, making it a county of vast cultures.

Cork City

Sitting proudly on an island in the middle of River Lee, the feel-good buzz of this city surges through hip coffee houses, breath taking art galleries, great museums and beating pubs. Hassle – free and a towny feel, Cork city is friendly and large on humour with great culinary delights to enjoy from a range of Michelin star restaurants. Leaf tours is in association and collaboration with restaurants offering package dining experiences to experience the best of Cork. The Cork city team will be awaiting upon your arrival and embark you on a journey around the city to visit a range of museums, pubs, coffee houses and restaurants. Any special request regarding food restrictions will be taken care of. Be ready to snap a bunch of pictures and become a food blogger! Cork city also offers a little adventure that is not to far away from the city itself, Blackrock castle observatory is located 2km out of Cork city. The castle was originally developed as a costal defence fortification with a medieval outlook. Currently it is still known to be the oldest surviving structure in use within Cork. If you are curious to find out more about this majestic castle, Leaf tours handles your entry and visit all-inclusive in the tour.
