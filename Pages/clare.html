Cliffs Of Moher

Ever wondered about the beautiful breath-taking sceneries and pictures you have seen on websites and wallpapers? Well think again! Leaf tours offers an amazing road trip adventure to county Clare to visit the Cliffs of Moher, located at the edge of the Burren region the sea cliffs are a distance of about 14km and are as high as 214 meters. The cliffs take their name from an old promontory fort called Mothar or Moher. This site is famous and receives almost 1 million visitors a year. The Cliffs of Moher Visitor Experience won an award in the Interpret Britain & Ireland Awards 2007 awarded by the Association of Heritage Interpretation (AHI). Be wowed with panoramic views from dramatic cliff edges. Walk along some of Ireland's most exhilarating coastal scenery. Grab a fireside seat at a traditional music session in the picturesque village of Doolin. Unmissable. The Cliffs of Moher have been long admired for their scenic beauty and is ancient folklore making it an icon and a place for a visit at least once in your life. Leaf tours handle private bus and car hires to visit this beautiful scenery with a personal tour guide that will journey with you along the cliffs to the best spots. Slip on your hiking shoes and be prepared for an adventure with Leaf tours!
