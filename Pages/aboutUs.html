About Us

First founded in 2022, Leaf tours has been moulded by the very definition of the believe in the four-leaf clover, representing love, faith, hope and luck to bring the best of Ireland to the people of the world! With great responsibility for delivering the best tourism experience one could offer of Irish history to the curious mind. Our tours are well organized to ensure that our members feel comfortable and prepared before the tour for a stress-free travel experience. Our services are offered to bring every individual an experience to remember, may it be the first-time visiting Ireland or to take a break from the hustle. Leaf tours ensures the convenience of safe travel with our team of approachable tour guides. All our tour guides are well equipped with knowledge and the history of Ireland. Leaf tours is in direct collaboration with other tourism members of the different counties within the region, making sure that all our tour members are well attended. Our team believes that all our tour members are given support throughout their journey with additional services upon request and if required.
